About boom beach

Boom Beach is an awesome game. You can build houses, sniper tower, sawmill and many more with woods or stones or iron.Also,you can upgrade things like sniper tower and it will become more stronger. You can also attack other people's hme base with your own troops. Sometimes you might lose but you can try it again but when you win you will recieve many resources such as wood ,gold and many more.

Resource base

In Boom Beach you will get a home base and a resource base. The resource base produces woods or stones When you win the resources base, you can set up the equipment wherever you want. But when other people win's your resources base, the level of the resources base increases,making it harder to destroy. So you should be care full when you set up your resource base as you might lose it.

The map. Here you can see the islands and your home base.

Why play Boom Beach?

When I first played Boom beach, it was very amazing.The islands the houses they were so cool. As I leveled up I received more things and I kept playing it. I think Boom Beach is a fantastic game because it helps kids think about strategy to win the islands.They have to analys the islands and make their home base difficult for others to destroy them. For me, Boom Beach is a very nice game.
