Boom Beach

  • The troops

    The troops are all very different from each other and are special. Example the heavy (the man who has bullets hanging on his shoulder) 's health is very high and he will not be easily defeated. If you are going to battle ,and you have heavy and rifleman or the zooka(the woman) ,you should send the heavy first followed by the rifleman or the zooka. The warrior (the man who has a hammer) is very good when there is about 10 or more of him. Warrior's attack is very strong so when there is about 10 of him they will destroy things very quickly.

  • Landing craft & Gun boat

    These are the landing craft (boats).These carry troops and as you upgrad it,it will carry more troops for battle. The gun boat is the big boat.It fires booms ,flares(guid troops) and more.
